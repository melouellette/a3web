MISK Consulting was founded in 2020 with the mission of providing clients with high quality implementation of Geographic Information Systems for half the cost

Our staff has extensive experience in the private, nonprofit and government sectors. We focus on aligning projects' business needs with effective GIS implementation by obtaining a clear understanding of project specific requirements and industry-standard best practices. Our custom systems, services, and products provide our clients straightforward and intuitive ways to interact with, visualize and analyze geographic data.
